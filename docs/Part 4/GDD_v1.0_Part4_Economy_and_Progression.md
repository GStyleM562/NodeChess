# Part 4 - Economy and Progression

Version: 1.0

Status: WORK IN PROGRESS

---

# 1. Philosophy

Economy exists to provide players with a sense of progression and collection.

Economy should never provide direct gameplay advantages.

Economy should encourage continued play.

Economy should reward engagement.

Economy should avoid Pay To Win mechanics.

Players should always feel they are progressing, even after losing matches.

Core Principles:

* No Pay To Win.
* No Gameplay Energy System.
* Cosmetics are encouraged.
* Collection is encouraged.
* PvP fairness is mandatory.
* PvE accessibility is mandatory.
